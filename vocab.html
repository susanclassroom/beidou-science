Topic 1 — GIGO Robotic Arm

機械手臂 | Assembly · Operation · Control

WORD 01
robotic arm
機械手臂
"Can you move the robotic arm to the left?"
WORD 02
assembly
組裝
"Let's start the assembly step by step."
WORD 03
components
零件 / 組件
"Check that all components are here."
WORD 04
joint
關節 / 連接處
"The joint lets the arm bend and rotate."
WORD 05
gripper
夾爪
"Use the gripper to pick up the object."
WORD 06
servo motor
伺服馬達
"The servo motor controls the arm's movement."
WORD 07
operate / operation
操作 / 操控
"I will operate the arm now."
WORD 08
controller

"Pass me the controller, please."
WORD 09

"Rotate the base 90 degrees to the right."
WORD 10

精確 / 精準度
"Good precision is key for a robotic arm."

💡 Pronunciation Tips for Filipino English

  • robot → ROH-bot  |  assemble → uh-SEM-bul  |  trajectory → truh-JEK-tuh-ree
  • pressure → PRESH-er  |  gravity → GRAV-ih-tee  |  variable → VAIR-ee-uh-bul
  • Filipino English speakers often use rising intonation for statements too — don't worry if it sounds like a question!
💬 Sentence Patterns — Say It in Science English!

10 key sentence frames to use during hands-on activities and group discussions.

1

How do you [verb] the [noun]?

詢問操作方式
How do you assemble the robotic arm? How do you launch the rocket?
2

Can you show me how to [verb] it?

請對方示範
Can you show me how to operate the controller? Can you show me how to measure the distance?
3

Let me try to [verb] it.

表達想嘗試
Let me try to rotate the arm. Let me try to adjust the angle.
4

My group is [verb+ing] the [noun].

描述小組正在做的事
My group is assembling the robotic arm. My group is testing different launch angles.
5

Be careful when you [verb].

給出安全提醒
Be careful when you use the gripper. Be careful when you launch the rocket.
6

What happens when you [verb] the [noun]?

詢問實驗結果
What happens when you increase the air pressure? What happens when you change the angle to 60°?
7

I think the [noun] affects [noun] because …

表達科學推論
I think the angle affects flight distance because … I think the motor affects arm speed because …
8

Our result shows that [finding].

報告實驗結果
Our result shows that 45° gives the longest distance. Our result shows that the arm can lift small objects.
9

In Taiwan, we also [verb] … Do you do this in the Philippines?

與國際夥伴交流文化
In Taiwan, we also learn STEM subjects. Do you have science clubs at your school?
10

That's interesting! In my country / school, we [fact].

分享與回應
That's interesting! In my school, we use GIGO kits for robotics. That's interesting! In Taiwan, we test three different angles.
