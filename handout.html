Handout 1 — GIGO Robotic Arm  ｜  機械手臂

Beidou JH · Bilingual Science Program · Philippines Exchange Prep

📺 Watch before you start: GIGO Robotic Arm — assembly, joints, and operation demonstration. 開始前先看：機械手臂組裝、關節與操作示範。
🔬 Science Concepts  科學概念

What is a Robotic Arm? 機械手臂是什麼？

A robotic arm is a programmable mechanical device that mimics the movement of a human arm. It uses servo motors and joints to grip, rotate, and move objects with precision.

機械手臂是模仿人類手臂動作的可程式化機械裝置，透過伺服馬達與關節進行精確夾取與移動。

How Does It Work? 它如何運作？

The controller sends signals to the servo motors. Each motor controls one joint. When all joints work together, the arm can pick up and place objects precisely.

控制器發送訊號到各伺服馬達，每個馬達控制一個關節，所有關節協同運作即可精準地夾取與放置物品。

Handout 2 — Air Paper Rocket  ｜  空氣紙火箭

Beidou JH · Bilingual Science Program · Philippines Exchange Prep

📺 Watch before you start: Paper Rockets and Launcher — construction, launch angles, and flight physics. 開始前先看：紙火箭製作、發射角度與飛行物理。
🔬 Science Concepts  科學概念

How Does an Air Rocket Fly? 空氣火箭如何飛行？

When you press the launcher, air pressure shoots the rocket forward. Gravity then pulls it back down. The shape of the fins and nose cone affect stability.

按下發射器時，氣壓推動火箭前進，重力再將其拉回地面。尾翼與箭頭的形狀影響飛行穩定性。

The Best Launch Angle  最佳發射角度

Mathematically, 45° gives the maximum range on flat ground. Below 45° = too flat, the rocket lands quickly. Above 45° = too steep, the rocket goes up but not far.

數學上，45° 在平地上可達最遠距離。低於45° 火箭很快落地；高於45° 火箭飛高卻飛不遠。

🌟 Science Facts to Share  可與國際夥伴分享的知識
📐
The 45° Rule

In ideal conditions, a 45° angle maximizes the range of any projectile — from paper rockets to real ones!

💨
Air Pressure Power

The harder you press, the more air pressure is created, giving the rocket a stronger push off the launcher.

🌍
Gravity at Work

Earth's gravity is 9.8 m/s². It pulls every object — even rockets — back to the ground at the same rate.

✈️
Fins = Stability

Fins at the rocket's tail keep it flying straight, just like the tail of an airplane or a badminton shuttlecock.
